## 01 Business Objectives & Challenges

### Objectives
- Increase **Average Order Value (AOV)**
- Increase **Online Conversion Rate**
- Improve **User Experience** across digital channels
- Reduce **Customer Acquisition Costs**

### Challenges
- Price sensitivity among target segments
- High cart & booking abandonment rates
- Navigation complexity & page load speed
- Ineffective audience targeting

## 02 SWOT Analysis

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Tier-based structure (Club → Elite VIP) with clear progression | Benefits vary significantly across regions |
| Free weekend rentals & complimentary upgrades | Limited partnerships vs. competitors |
| Seamless digital access via mobile app | Low member awareness & benefit underutilisation |
| Partnership with Accor Hotels & Priority Pass | No airline integration reducing perceived value |

| Opportunities | Threats |
|---------------|---------|
| Expand global partnerships (airlines, hotels, platforms) | Intense competition: Hertz Gold Plus, Avis Preferred, Sixt |
| Leverage AI for personalised rewards & communications | Economic downturns reducing rental frequency |
| Eco-friendly rewards for sustainability-conscious customers | Rapid technology advances in competitor programmes |
| Promotional campaigns to boost programme awareness | New entrants with disruptive subscription-based models |

## 04 Consumer Behaviour & Psychology

Psychological principles integrated into the Privilege Programme to drive engagement, loyalty, and member satisfaction:

- **Behavioural Conditioning** — Analyse rental patterns to offer personalised rewards, reinforcing repeat bookings
- **Self-Determination Theory** — CRM tools personalise experiences around autonomy, competence & relatedness
- **Social Identity** — Showcase testimonials to create community belonging and aspirational tier progression
- **Cognitive Dissonance** — Ensure members feel validated in choosing Europcar, reducing post-purchase doubt
- **Feedback Loop** — Real-time feedback systems improve responsiveness and member satisfaction dynamically
- **Elaboration Likelihood** — Clear benefit communications drive central (thoughtful) information processing

## 06 A/B Testing Strategy & Roadmap

**Tool:** A/B Testing, Feature Flags, Personalisation & Predictive Targeting Platform

### 6-Step Experimentation Process

```
01 Objective ID → 02 Audience Segmentation → 03 Variation Creation
→ 04 Experimentation Launch → 05 Simulation & Analytics → 06 Implement Winner
```

### Key A/B Testing Use Cases

| Test Area | Hypothesis | Expected Outcome |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| **Booking Funnel** | Redesign form fields & reduce friction in payment step | ↑ Booking completion rate, ↓ abandonment |
| **Landing Pages** | Personalise post-login page for new vs. returning members | ↑ CTR, ↑ session depth |
| **Discount Targeting** | Show offers only to members with active booking intent | ↑ ROI ×3 |
| **CTA Optimisation** | Test button colour & copy ('Redeem Now' vs. 'Claim Your Benefit') | ↑ Click-through rate |
| **Loyalty Tier Upsell** | Widget showing points gap to next tier at checkout | ↑ AOV, ↑ tier upgrade rate |
| **Personalised Emails** | Tier-personalised emails with dynamic points balance | ↑ Email open rate, ↑ revenue per email |

### KPIs Monitored
`Click Through Rate` · `Time on Site >600s` · `Conversion Rate` · `Number of Visits` · `Cross-sell Rate` · `Campaign ROI`

## 08 Strategic Recommendations

| Priority | Action | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| 🔴 HIGH | **Personalisation** | Deploy predictive AI engine to deliver individualised rewards and booking funnel experiences by tier and rental behaviour |
| 🔴 HIGH | **Expand Partnership Ecosystem** | Integrate airline loyalty programmes (Air France, BA, Lufthansa) and hotel chains beyond Accor |
| 🟡 MED | **Eco-Friendly Reward Tracks** | Introduce a sustainability tier (e.g., EV rental bonus points) to capture the green-travel segment |
| 🟡 MED | **Continuous A/B Testing Programme** | Run structured experimentation cycles on booking funnel, loyalty dashboard UX and coupon targeting |
| 🟢 LOW | **Cultural Service Localisation** | Tailor customer service scripts and reward mechanics to country-specific cultural expectations |
| 🟢 LOW | **Member Awareness Campaign** | Targeted onboarding emails and in-app benefit highlights to reduce benefit underutilisation |
